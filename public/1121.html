What is 1121 basmati steam rice?

What is the difference between 1121 and 1509 rice?

1509 rice is cheaper in comparison to 1121 rice variety because 1509 rice's cultivation time is less and produces higher yield as compared to 1121 rice. Upon cooking, the grains exhibit great elongation, excellent fluffiness, are non-sticky and have a pleasant aroma.


Which is the longest rice?

The variety of basmati rice was developed by Padma Shri awardee Devesh Mittal and his brother Aman Mittal. This variety of rice is known for its extraordinary kernel (grain) length, which can be as much as 8.4 millimetres (0.33 in) for a single grain, the longest-ever known released cultivar in the world.

What is sella rice?

Golden Sella Basmati Rice is a cultivar of basmati rice that has been parboiled or partially boiled while still in its husk and then milled. So Golden Sella is actually parboiled basmati rice. The rice turns pale yellow after parboiling hence the name 'Golden'



What is the difference between Sella rice and steam rice?

The husk is removed easily on steaming the rice; therefore the chances of damaging of seed coat and tissues are lessened. During processing, this rice retains more of the vitamins and minerals from the original grain rice. Length of Sella basmati rice basically about 8.30 mm.
